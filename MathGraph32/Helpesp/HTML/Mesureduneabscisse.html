Abscissa measure

Numerical objects ›› Measures ››

You can measure the abscissa of a point on a line relative to two points through icon.


This abscissa will not exist if the points are nor aligned.


In order to measure abscissa of M relative to (A;B) :

  • Click on A (point of abscissa 0).
  • Click on B (point of abscissa 1).
  • Click on M to measure abscissa of M (M must be aligned with avec A and B).


The three points clicked must be named.

If they aren't they will be named by the tool.


In future calculus, this abscissa will be represented by M(A,B).
