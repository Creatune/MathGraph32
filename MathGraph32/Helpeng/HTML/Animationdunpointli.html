Linked point animation macro

Macros ›› Different kind of macros ››

A linked point animation macro is created through menu Macros - New macro - Linked point animation.

It's purpose is to move automatically a linked point of the figure.

The animation starts by clicking on the button of the macro.

Press on Escape key to stop animation.
