Macros

MathGraph32 macros are buttons that fire an action on the figure when pressed.

This kind of button starts with ⇒ character.


Some macros manage a list of objects. It is possible to remove or add objects to such macros through menus Macros - Add objects to macro or Macro - Remove objects from macro.


Once created, a macro ca be modified or reclassified through menu Macros - Modification or reclassification.
